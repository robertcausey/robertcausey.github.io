AVS 303 - Equine Management Cooperative

The following 5 assignments are require to complete AVS 303.

Lectures:

AVS 196/303 Lecture Quiz 1 - Integument, endocrine, digestive tract, environment

AVS 196/303 Lecture Quiz 2 - Points of the horse, anatomy, lameness examination and gaits.

AVS 196/303 Lecture Quiz 3 - Lameness therapy, infectious diseases and vaccinations

Labs:

Students attending labs at Witter are not required to take complete these 4 online lab assignments, but may complete these as an optional exercise for review of the material. Students taking the class remotely should complete lab 1 (Horse Handling) and lab 3 (Lameness observation and examination). Labs 2 (Longeing and Gaits) and 4 (Bandage bloopers) are for self assessment only and are not required.

AVS 303 Lab 1 - Horse Handling - Required if you are taking class remotely.

AVS 303 Lab 2 - Longeing and gaits - Not required. For self assessment only

AVS 303 Lab 3 - Lameness Observation and examination - Required if you are taking the class remotely

AVS 303 Lab 4 - Bandage Bloopers - Not required. For self-assessment only.
