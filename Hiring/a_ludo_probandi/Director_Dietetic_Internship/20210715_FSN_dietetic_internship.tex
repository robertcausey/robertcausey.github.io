This is an essential service in meeting Maine's workforce needs in health care. The Accreditation Council for Education in Nutrition and Dietetics (ACEND) is the accrediting agency for the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics, and confers eligibility on our students to sit for the National Registration Examination. The Dietetic Internship Program is a two-year program, with both masterâ€™s and doctoral programs available.
