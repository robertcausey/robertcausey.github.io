Horse Handling when taking care of the horses at the Witter Center

This is a list of the basic horse handling videos. We require some form of head protection when moving horses between paddocks and stalls, such as a construction type helmet or riding helmet. On the ground we do not require a chin strap to be fastened so long as the helmet fits snugly. However, when riding, an ASTM approved riding helmet must be worn, and the chin strap secured.
